About

Collecting and responding to user feedback is what makes agile software development lean. This relationship creates a constant need to release early and develop with users in mind. As a result, user feedback is crucial to understanding how to add value to a product.

User experience (UX) is what entices clients to try your product first. And User interface design plays a key part in your UX plans. Rapid prototyping helps you make an intelligent guess and then keep fine-tuning to meet your clients' needs.
